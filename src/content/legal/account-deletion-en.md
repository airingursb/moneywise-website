# Delete your MoneyWise account

**App:** MoneyWise · **Developer:** Airing · **Effective:** 2026-05-01

This page explains how to permanently delete your MoneyWise account and what happens to your data.

## Two ways to request account deletion

### Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

1. Open **MoneyWise** on your phone.
2. Tap the **Profile** tab (or open **Settings**).
3. Tap your profile card at the top → **My Account**.
4. Scroll to **Danger Zone** → tap **Delete Account**.
5. Read the confirmation dialog, then type **DELETE** to confirm.
6. Tap **Delete Account** again. The app signs you out and returns to the login screen.

The deletion completes immediately. There is no email confirmation step — once you confirm in the app, your account is gone.

### Option 2 — Email request

If you cannot access the app (for example, you uninstalled it but still want your server-side data removed), email **moflowapp@gmail.com** from the address tied to your MoneyWise account, with the subject **"Delete my MoneyWise account"**.

We will verify ownership and complete the deletion within 14 days.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following are **permanently and irreversibly removed**:

**Server-side (cloud):**
- Your MoneyWise authentication identity (email + linked Google / Apple Sign-In).
- All cloud-synced rows: accounts, transactions, budgets, investments, recurring rules, and category settings.
- Receipt and attachment images stored on our servers.
- Your avatar image.
- Your user preferences, including any AI provider API key you saved with cloud sync.
- Your membership in any shared family workspaces.

**On your device:**
- Your local session is cleared and the app is signed out.
- The local encrypted SQLite database remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear local data — this is yours, not on our servers, and we cannot reach it. To erase local data immediately: delete the app from your phone.

**Third-party links:**
- Google or Apple Sign-In links to your MoneyWise account are unlinked from our side. The token your Google or Apple account holds expires naturally; you can also revoke it manually in your Google account settings or Apple ID settings.

## What is retained, and for how long

- **Family workspaces you owned**: if you owned a shared family workspace with other members, that workspace's data is preserved for **30 days** so other members can copy out anything they need. After 30 days the family data is permanently deleted.
- **Anonymized analytics events**: aggregate counts of in-app actions (page views, button taps) collected before deletion remain in our analytics pipeline. These are not linked to your identity after the deletion completes — they stay only as anonymous totals.
- **Nothing else.** We do not retain backup copies of your transactions, balances, or AI history beyond the family-grace case above.

## Active subscriptions are NOT cancelled by account deletion

Deleting your MoneyWise account does **not** cancel any active App Store or Google Play subscription. To stop billing:

- **iPhone / iPad:** open Settings → tap your name → **Subscriptions** → MoneyWise → **Cancel Subscription**.
- **Android:** open Google Play Store → tap your profile → **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions** → MoneyWise → **Cancel**.

Cancel your subscription **before** deleting your account if you want to stop billing.
